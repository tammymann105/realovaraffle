How to Play

  • Enter your Name & Email, stake between €1–€500.
  • Pick exactly 6 unique numbers (1–100).
  • Pay via Debit/Credit or PayPal.
  • Wait 2 minutes — draws run every 2 minutes on a rolling schedule.
  • After the draw, if you match 3 or more numbers, you’re a winner:
    • 3 matches → 4× your stake
    • 4 matches → 6× your stake
    • 5 matches → 8× your stake
    • 6 matches → 10× your stake
  • Winners can “Claim Prize” and fill in bank details for a SEPA transfer arriving in 1–2 business days.
